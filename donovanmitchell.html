Donovan Mitchell

About Donovan

Donovan Mitchell was born on September 7th 1996 in Elmsford, New York. He played for the University of Louisville and was drafted into the NBA in 2017. He plays for the utah jazz and is incredibly awsome. He wears number 45 because it was Michael Jordans number when he played baseball. Donovan went on to win the Slam Dunk Contest at the NBA allstar weekend his rookie year.
